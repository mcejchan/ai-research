## Key Takeaways

### 1. The Rapid Growth of AI
**Quote:** "The AI companies are getting bigger faster than anything we've ever seen."  
**Why it matters:** The unprecedented speed of AI growth presents significant investment opportunities. As AI infrastructure expands, it creates a robust environment for new companies to thrive, potentially leading to substantial market cap increases over the next decade.

### 2. Declining Input Costs and Improving Quality
**Quote:** "The cost of the inputs... has declined 99% or a little more than 99% over the last two years."  
**Why it matters:** The dramatic reduction in input costs, coupled with rapid improvements in model capabilities, allows for innovative applications of AI. This trend can lead to enhanced efficiencies and new business models, making AI more accessible and valuable across various sectors.

### 3. Market Opportunity Exceeds Previous Cycles
**Quote:** "AI is going to be much larger because I think the impact on the economy is going to be much larger."  
**Why it matters:** The potential economic impact of AI surpasses that of previous tech cycles, such as mobile and cloud computing. With AI's integration into various industries, the market opportunity is vast, promising significant returns for investors and businesses alike.

## Practical Tips & Procedures

### Evaluating AI Business Models
**What it does:** Helps assess the viability and potential success of AI companies.  
**Steps:**
1. Analyze the gross retention rate to determine customer satisfaction and loyalty.
2. Evaluate the ease of customer acquisition through organic demand and pricing strategies.
3. Monitor the expansion of usage among existing customers to gauge long-term value.
**Quote:** "We look for things where like 90% plus customers are sticking around."  
**When to use:** Use this evaluation framework when considering investments in AI startups or assessing the health of existing AI businesses.
