## Practical Tips & Procedures

### Creating Effective Flashcards
**What it does:** Helps organize and retain knowledge efficiently.
**Steps:**
1. Identify a piece of knowledge or fact you want to remember.
2. Create a flashcard using a simple template that includes the question on one side and the answer on the other.
3. Include a source or context for the information to evoke memories associated with it.
**Quote:** "Always put a source in when you create a flash card; it’s nice to know where the information came from."
**When to use:** Use this method whenever you encounter new information that you want to retain for future reference.
