# How Andrew Wilkinson Uses Opus 4.5 in His Work and Life

## Key Takeaways

### 1. The Power of AI Automation
**Quote:** "I literally feel like I have like 30 free employees and they're just working 24/7 and I'm paying them like $40 a day."  
**Why it matters:** Andrew emphasizes how AI tools, particularly Cloud Code, have transformed his productivity, allowing him to automate tasks that would traditionally require a large team. This shift signifies a new era in software development where automation can drastically reduce costs and increase efficiency.

### 2. Building Personal Tools with AI
**Quote:** "I built this tool that tells you the problems in your relationship and how to solve them."  
**Why it matters:** Andrew's creation of a relationship analysis tool showcases the potential for AI to provide personalized insights. This not only highlights the versatility of AI applications but also demonstrates how individuals can leverage technology to enhance personal relationships.

### 3. The Future of Programming
**Quote:** "There will be no programmers, not in the way that we understand them in 2 or 3 years."  
**Why it matters:** Andrew predicts a significant shift in the programming landscape, suggesting that traditional coding roles may become obsolete as AI tools evolve. This insight is crucial for professionals in the tech industry to consider their future roles and adapt accordingly.

**What it does:** Creates a personalized email triage system to manage high volumes of emails efficiently.  
**Steps:**
