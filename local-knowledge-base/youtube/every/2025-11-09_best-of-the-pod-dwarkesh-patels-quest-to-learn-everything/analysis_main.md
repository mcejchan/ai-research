## Practical Tips & Procedures

### Using AI for Active Learning
**What it does:** Enhances comprehension and retention of complex topics through interactive questioning.  
**Steps:**
1. Identify key concepts from the material you are studying.
2. Use an AI tool (like Claude) to generate questions and prompts based on those concepts.
3. Engage with the AI to clarify doubts and reinforce understanding through spaced repetition techniques.
**Quote:** "I can just... continue the conversation with Claude and have it explain what's going on."  
**When to use:** When studying difficult subjects or preparing for interviews where deep understanding is required.
