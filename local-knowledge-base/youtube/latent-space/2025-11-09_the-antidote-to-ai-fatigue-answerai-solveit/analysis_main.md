## Practical Tips & Procedures

### Step-by-Step Iterative Development
**What it does:** Facilitates the development of software through small, manageable steps.  
**Steps:**
1. Identify a specific task or problem to solve.
2. Break the task into smaller components or steps.
3. Implement the first step and gather immediate feedback.
4. Adjust based on feedback and proceed to the next step.
**Quote:** "The more often you do that, the worse the dialogue answers get because it's in the training data when it’s seen mistakes."  
**When to use:** Use this approach in software development, writing, or any project requiring iterative improvement.
