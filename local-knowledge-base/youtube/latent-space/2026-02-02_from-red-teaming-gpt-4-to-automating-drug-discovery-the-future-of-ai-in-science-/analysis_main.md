## Practical Tips & Procedures

### Automating Scientific Discovery
**What it does:** Streamlines the process of hypothesis generation, experimentation, and analysis using AI.  
**Steps:**
1. Use AI models to generate hypotheses based on existing data.
2. Select experiments to validate these hypotheses.
3. Analyze experimental results and refine hypotheses accordingly.  
**Quote:** "We want to automate that sort of loop."  
**When to use:** Ideal for researchers looking to enhance efficiency in experimental design and data analysis.
