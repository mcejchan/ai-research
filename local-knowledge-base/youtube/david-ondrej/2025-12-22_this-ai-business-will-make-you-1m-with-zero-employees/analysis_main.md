**Quote:** "Choosing the right business model is like choosing the right fishing spot."

## Practical Tips & Procedures

### Building an AI Software Company
**What it does:** Guides you on how to create a scalable AI software business.  
**Steps:**
1. Identify a problem that can be solved with AI.
2. Use AI tools to develop your software, leveraging AI agents for coding.
3. Launch your software and continuously improve it based on user feedback and AI advancements.  
**Quote:** "Building an AI software company is the key to creating a scalable 100k per year business."  
**When to use:** This approach is ideal for individuals looking to enter the AI space without extensive coding experience.
